Our Policies

At ServiCell, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality service and support. Our policies are designed to ensure transparency, fairness, and customer satisfaction.

The Fine Print (Made Simple)

Picking Up Your Gear

We know life gets busy! We’ll notify you as soon as your device is ready. However, we have limited shelf space, so please try to pick up your device within 90 days. After that, we’ll consider it abandoned so we can recover the cost of the parts and labor we put into it.

Your Data is Precious

We treat your photos and files with the utmost respect. That said, repairs can be unpredictable. To stay safe, please back up your device before bringing it in. ServiCell isn't liable for data loss, but we’ll always do our best to protect what’s yours.

Our Responsibility

While your device is in our care, we treat it like our own. Once that 90-day window passes, however, we can no longer be responsible for the device or any data on it. Help us help you by staying in touch!

Payment & Fair Play

To keep our prices fair for everyone, we require all service fees to be settled before you take your device home. We don't like holding onto your tech any longer than we have to!

The Handshake

By leaving your device with us, you’re saying "I’m cool with these terms." If we ever need to update these rules, we’ll post them right here immediately.

If You Supply the Parts

We’re happy to help you install a screen or part you’ve already bought elsewhere! To keep things fair, here is how that works:

  • Labor Only: When you bring your own part, you are only paying us for the service of installing it. We aren't the sellers of that part, so we can't guarantee the functionality or quality of the hardware itself.
  • Defective Parts: If the screen you brought us turns out to be a "lemon" (dead pixels, touch issues, etc.), you’ll need to handle the return or exchange with the original person who sold it to you.
  • Double the Work: If a part you supplied fails and you want us to swap it for a new one, a second labor fee will apply. We want to get it right the first time, but we can't do the work twice for free!

Pro-Tip: Want a "Full Package" guarantee? Buy your parts directly from ServiCell. When we provide the part AND the repair, we take 100% responsibility for both!
