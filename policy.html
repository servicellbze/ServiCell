Service Policies

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

At ServiCell, we believe in transparent repairs. These policies ensure we provide the best care for your technology and keep our prices fair for everyone in Belize City.

1 Before the Repair

Backup Your Data

Repairs can be unpredictable. Please back up your device before drop-off. ServiCell is not liable for data loss, though we prioritize data safety in every repair.

2 During the Repair

Water Resistance

Opening a factory-sealed device breaks the original waterproof bond. While we reseal your phone with industrial adhesive, we cannot guarantee original water-resistance ratings.

Customer-Supplied Parts

  • Labor Only: We warranty the installation work, but not the part itself if it was not sourced by us.
  • Defective Parts: If your part fails, you must handle the warranty with your original seller.
  • Double Labor: If a customer-supplied part fails, a second labor fee applies for re-installation.
ServiCell Pro-Tip: Want a full peace-of-mind guarantee? Buy your parts directly from us. When we provide both the part and the labor, we take 100% responsibility!
3 After the Repair

The 7-Day Test Drive

You have a 7-day window to report any issues with the installed part. Bring it back and we’ll make it right.

4 Pickup & Abandonment

90-Day Storage Rule

Devices not picked up within 90 days of the "Ready" notification will be considered abandoned and may be recycled to recover costs.
