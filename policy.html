Service Policies

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

At ServiCell, we believe in transparent repairs. These policies ensure we provide the best care for your technology and keep our prices fair for everyone in Belize City.

1 Before the Repair

Backup Your Data

Repairs can be unpredictable. Please back up your device before drop-off. ServiCell is not liable for data loss, though we prioritize data safety in every repair.

2 During the Repair

Water Resistance

Opening a factory-sealed device breaks the original waterproof bond. While we reseal your phone with industrial adhesive, we cannot guarantee original water-resistance ratings.

Customer-Supplied Parts

Weʼre happy to help you install a screen or part youʼve already bought elsewhere! To keep things fair, here is how that works:

  • Labor Only: When you bring your own part, you are only paying us for the service of installing it. We aren't the sellers of that part, so we can't guarantee the functionality or quality of the hardware itself.
  • Defective Parts: If the screen you brought us turns out to be a "lemon" (dead pixels, touch issues, etc.), youʼll need to handle the return or exchange with the original person who sold it to you.
  • Double Labor: If a part you supplied fails and you want us to swap it for a new one, a second labor fee will apply. We want to get it right the first time, but we can't do the work twice for free!
ServiCell Pro-Tip: Want a full peace-of-mind guarantee? Buy your parts directly from us. When we provide both the part and the labor, we take 100% responsibility!
3 After the Repair

The 3-Day Test Drive

For all ServiCell-provided parts, you have a 3-day window to report any functional issues. If something isn't perfect, bring it back and we’ll make it right at no extra cost.

Note: While we stand by our installation, third-party parts sourced elsewhere are excluded from this 3-day replacement window. If a customer-supplied part fails, a new labor fee will apply for the swap.

4 Pickup & Responsibility

Picking Up Your Gear

We'll notify you as soon as your device is ready. We know life gets busy, but please try to pick it up within 90 days. After that, it will be considered abandoned so we can recover repair costs.

ServiCell Pro-Tip: Don't miss the 90-day pickup deadline! You can check your repair status with our Live Repair Tracking 🔍 anytime. Just use the ticket number we gave you at drop-off!

90-Day Storage Rule

While your device is in our care, we treat it like our own. However, once that 90-day window passes, we can no longer be responsible for the device or any data on it

5 Final Agreement

The Handshake

By leaving your device with us, you're basically saying, "I'm cool with these terms." If we ever update anything, we'll post it right here immediately.

Still Have Questions?

If anything here feels confusing—or you've got a unique situation—don't hesitate to reach out. We believe in clear communication and are always happy to explain how things work before we ever touch your device.
