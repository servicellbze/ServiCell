Device Storage & Liability Policy

Devices left for repair or service are the responsibility of the customer. Devices unclaimed for over 30 days from the service completion date may be considered abandoned. ServiCell reserves the right to dispose of, recycle, or otherwise handle any device left beyond this period.

Liability Disclaimer

ServiCell is not responsible for any loss, damage, or data on devices left beyond the storage period. While devices are in our care, we take reasonable precautions to protect your property; however, we are not liable for unforeseen incidents such as fire, theft, natural disaster, or accidental damage.

Data Responsibility

Customers are strongly encouraged to back up their data prior to service. ServiCell is not responsible for data loss during repair, service, or storage.

Communication

Customers will be notified via provided contact information when the device is ready for pickup. It is the customer's responsibility to update contact information to ensure proper communication.

Payment & Fees

All repair and service fees must be settled before pickup. Storage beyond 30 days may incur additional storage fees, as determined by ServiCell.

Legal

By leaving a device with ServiCell, the customer agrees to these terms. ServiCell reserves the right to update this policy at any time, with updates communicated via our website and/or on receipt forms.

Policy Changes

ServiCell reserves the right to modify, update, or change any of these policies at any time without prior notice. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. We encourage customers to review this policy page regularly to stay informed of any updates. Continued use of our services following any policy changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
