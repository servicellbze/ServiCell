Our Story

Founded in 2014 by a single passionate technician, ServiCell began with a simple mission: to fix electronics reliably without breaking the bank. What started as a personal hobby has since grown into a tight-knit team of 2–3 dedicated technicians. At ServiCell, we believe every device deserves a second chance, and every customer deserves transparency, care, and respect.
