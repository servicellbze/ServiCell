Our Story

Founded in 2014 by a single passionate technician, ServiCell began with a simple mission: to fix electronics reliably without breaking the bank. What started as a mere hobby has since grown into a tight-knit team of 2–3 dedicated technicians, each committed to delivering fast, professional, and affordable tech solutions. Over the years, we've expanded our expertise to cover phones, laptops, gaming consoles, and software support, while maintaining the personal touch that made us trusted from day one. At ServiCell, we believe every device deserves a second chance, and every customer deserves transparency, care, and respect. Whether it's a cracked screen, a stubborn laptop, or a software issue, we're here to get you back online with confidence.
